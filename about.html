My Story

Passionate About Data,
Driven by Automation

Hello! I'm Sumit Kumar, a Data Analyst and MIS Automation Specialist based in New Delhi. With a strong foundation in commerce and hands-on expertise in data tools, I thrive at the intersection of business intelligence and technology.

My journey started with a Bachelor of Commerce from the University of Delhi, which gave me a deep understanding of business processes. I then expanded into the technical world through data analytics training at Ducat and MIS Automation specialization at BCI — combining business acumen with technical skill.

At Nutex Apparel, I built and deployed multiple internal systems — from inventory management to WhatsApp API integrations — that streamlined operations and reduced manual work significantly. I genuinely love finding inefficiencies and automating them away.

Before that, two years in telesales at Godiva Bioadvances taught me the value of clear communication, resilience, and truly understanding what customers need — skills that now make me a better analyst and collaborator.
